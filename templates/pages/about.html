Our Story

Why We Built CuraSuite

Healthcare in India is undergoing rapid digital transformation. Yet most clinics and labs still use fragmented, outdated software — or no software at all. Reception runs on paper registers. Appointments are managed on WhatsApp. Billing happens in Excel.

We saw first-hand how much time healthcare professionals were losing to administrative tasks that technology should handle automatically. A doctor spending 30 minutes on billing is a doctor who can't spend those 30 minutes with patients.

CuraSuite was born from a simple conviction: Indian healthcare providers deserve the same quality digital tools that hospitals in the US and UK take for granted — at a price that makes sense for a solo practitioner in Dehradun or a pathology lab in Surat.

Four years later, we've helped 500+ clinics, doctors, and labs across 15 cities transform their practices. We're just getting started.
