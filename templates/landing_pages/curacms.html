How It Works

From First Call to Live Site in 7–14 Days

No agencies, no back-and-forth over email — a guided process built specifically for clinics.

1. Discovery Call

A free 30-minute call to learn your specialty, current pain points, and what "done" looks like for your clinic.

2. Blueprint & Design

We map your pages, branding, and which of the 12 modules your practice actually needs — no generic template.

3. Build & Configure

Our team builds it out — doctor profiles, treatments, booking flow, and the AI assistant — while you keep seeing patients.

4. Review & Refine

You get a private preview link and request changes — we polish every detail before anything goes public.

Your site goes live in 7–14 days — fully indexed, booking-ready, with our team and AI assistant backing you up after.
